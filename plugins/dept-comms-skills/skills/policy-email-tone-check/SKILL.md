# Policy email tone check
When given a draft email, assess it for:
- **Tone** — professional and supportive, never condescending to a junior reader.
- **Clarity** — one ask per paragraph; define any policy jargon on first use.
- **Appropriateness** — flag anything that should go through HR or Legal instead.
Return a short list of concrete suggested edits, then a cleaned-up version of
the email. If the content looks like it needs an escalation (HR/legal matter),
say so and stop.
